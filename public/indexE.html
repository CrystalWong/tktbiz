The China Internet Security Conference (ISC) was launched in 2013 and has been successfully held for the five time. The first Chinese Internet Conference has triggered an unprecedented enthusiasm for "Internet Security". In 2014, more than twenty thousand people were recorded in the number of participants, the first U.S. Department of Homeland Security Minister Tom Ridge, the father of computer virus Fred Cohen, and other top experts and scholars from home and abroad. The convening of the conference led to the full swing of the domestic security concept stocks. 2015 ISC is an unprecedented scale with a total attendance of over thirty thousand people. The theme of the conference "data driven security" has become the industry's recognized safety concept and development trend. In 2016, more than 160 security leaders and experts from 7 countries, 16 well-known universities, more than 20 scientific research institutions and 31 security manufacturers were attended by the theme of "cooperative linkage, Co Construction of Security + destiny community". The 2017 ISC from the 249 security industry think tanks and industry security experts from 10 countries made a 212 keynote speech on the theme of "all things are the yardstick of security", and nearly 40 thousand network security practitioners attended the meeting, and hundreds of millions of people watched online. ISC has become the largest and most influential safety event in Asia Pacific region.
